MY FAVORITE PAST-TIME

Immersing myself in the art of drawing


The art of drawing is beyond replicating the image in our head or an object. To me, drawing is to use my creativity and change things along the process of drawing to portray a more aesthetic image. Through art, I managed to overcome personal issues such as impatience. Shifting my focus from doing things correctly to doing things well while enjoying the process.
